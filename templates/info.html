Welcome to the Dataplotter tool!


This analyzes the data a tensile strain test gives you.

To use, upload a .csv file with your raw data from the Instron machine, which should have 3 columns for time, Extension (mm), and Force (N). Also enter the area and the thickness (the width) of your sample; please DO NOT enter units, just the numbers, or else it won't run. (Default settings are 3.81E-7 for area and 0.01 for thickness.)

Then hit "submit". You will get a plot with the Stress (MPa) vs. Strain, Young's modulus, and the standard deviation across all the files you upload. If you only upload one file, standard deviation would be 0.0.

Have fun analyzing data!
